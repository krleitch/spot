About
Spot is a social media website which allows you to share your ideas anonymously and at the same time with the friends you choose.
Be Anonymous
All spots and comments you make are anonymous by default. If you want to share with friends, @tag them in a comment and only they will know who you are. If they tag you back then you will know each other while still remaining anonymous to everyone else. If you are the owner of a spot your profile picture will be the spy above to let everyone else know you made the Spot.
See What's Happening Around You
location_city
You can only comment on Spots that are in a 10 mile radius, or if you are out of range only in a comment chain you were tagged in. Sort by home or global to see what's happening around you and in the world. Your location information will always be private and will never be given away to anyone for any reason.
