About
Spot is a social media website which allows you to share your ideas anonymously and at the same time with the friends you choose.
Be Anonymous
  • All spots and comments you make are anonymous by default.
  • If you want to share with friends, @tag them in a comment and only they will know who you are.
  • If you are tagged back then you will know each other while still remaining anonymous to everyone else.
  • Spot owners will be marked with the spy profile photo as seen above.
See Events Around You
location_city
  • You can only comment on Spots that are in a 10 mile radius.
  • If you are out of range you can only comment in chains you are tagged.
  • Sort by home or global to see what's happening around you or in the world.
  • Your location will always be private and will never be given away to anyone for any reason.
