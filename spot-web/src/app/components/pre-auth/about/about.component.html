What is Spot?
Spot is a social media website which allows you to share your ideas anonymously and with your own chosen network of friends.
Be Anonymous
All posts and comments you make are anonymous. If you want to let friends know who you are, tag them in a comment and they will know its you. If they tag you back then you can know each other while remaining anonymous to everyone else
See whats happening in your Area
You can only comment on spots that are in your area, or you were tagged in with afriend. Sort by local to see whats happening around you and get involved with your community. Your location will never be given away and you will always be anonymous as long as you choose to be.
