What Is Spot?
Spot is a social media tool for sharing your thoughts anonymously! Your account information and location will never be given away.
Be Anonymous
For every Spot you comment on you will be given a unique icon that will represent you in that thread. This icon is different for each Spot.
Share With Your Friends
Spot allows you to tag your friends to share spots. When you tag a friend, they will receive your username. If they tag you back then you can both secretly know who each other are while staying anonymous to those you don't want to see.
Spots In Your Area
Sort locally to see what is happening in your area. Spot needs your location to do this. Spot will never give your location away to anybody.
